Recipe for Gnocchi

Gnocchi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) potato gnocchi
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the potato gnocchi according to the package instructions or until they float to the surface (usually around 2-3 minutes). Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked gnocchi to the skillet. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, tossing gently until the gnocchi are lightly browned.
  4. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the gnocchi. Stir to combine and let it melt into a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the gnocchi hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.
